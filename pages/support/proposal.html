Topic Proposal

Our Topic - Cyber Crime and Large-scale Intrusions

We chose this topic because we are passionate about cyber security and understand the impact that it has on our modern world. More than ever, the average person is at risk of being affected by large-scale cyber crime as they surrender their personal information and rely heavily on the services and applications of major corporations. This topic is relevant to us as we are still affected by the large-scale intrusions. We use the applications, and our own data is stored in these companies. It also is quite relevant to us since we are planning on working in the cyber security field. These large-scale intrusions also affect us as these large companies that are being targeted are the things that are incredibly crucial to our day-to-day life. Examples of these are big companies such as Facebook and social media companies getting targeted containing all our personal information and conversations with people.
